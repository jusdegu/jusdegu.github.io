About Me

Hello world!

I have a strong background in C and C++ due to the majority of FSU courses utilizing this as their programming language. Additional programming languages that I am familiar with are Java and JavaScript.

When I'm not programming, my hobbies include gaming, cooking, and fishing.

  • Embedded Systems development: A future hobby that I'm very much interested in: possibly building some form of system using a series of computers or microcontrollers (such as Raspberry Pis or Arduinos).
